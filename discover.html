Discover — UMD & Duluth

Why I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth

I initially chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because many of my friends were attending. Even though I no longer live with any of them, I’ve formed great friendships with new roommates and classmates. One of the strongest qualities of UMD is the sense of community — being a smaller school makes it easier to build close connections and meet a large portion of students in your class.

What I like about Duluth

I also appreciate Duluth’s location. While I’m not someone who spends a lot of time outdoors, I still enjoy how much the city has to offer. Whether you’re spending time in Canal Park, visiting the mall, or exploring the area, there’s always something to do. Duluth has a unique atmosphere that makes living here enjoyable.

Personal experiences

Some of my favorite experiences at UMD include going to the bar with friends and playing intramural sports like basketball and hockey. Since UMD is a big hockey school, I also enjoy going to games at Amsoil Arena and supporting the team. I appreciate the variety of food options on campus as well — having a block plan the past few years has helped me save money while keeping meals convenient.
